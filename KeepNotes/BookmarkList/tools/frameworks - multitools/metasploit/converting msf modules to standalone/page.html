One of the easiest ways to get a Metasploit exploit to standalone is to edit the module directly.

Step 1: remove all socket connections, you won't be needing them
Step 2: use:
  print_status(Rex::Text.to_hex(<VARIABLE>))
to print out the exact hex of what metasploit is sending to the target
Step 3: copy the hex, load into your favorite template, paste, and go!

You can do this for the full payload (let MSF handle payload/encoding/etc) or you can do it just for the initial bytes/trigger and paste in your own shellcode manually
